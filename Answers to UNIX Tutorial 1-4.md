<h1>Answers for Unix Tutorial - Lab 1</h1>

<h3>Exercise 1b</h3>
Q: What do you think (ls ~) would list?
A: The contents of the user's home directory

Q: What do you think (ls ~/..) would list?
A: The contents of the parent directory of user's home directory (/home)
